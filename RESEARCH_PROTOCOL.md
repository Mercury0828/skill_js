- **2026-06-10 — Immediate skill migration (Jason).** The moment a generalizable lesson/technique of value is discovered during project work, add it to skill_js AND push — do not wait for phase boundaries. Phase boundaries remain a sweep checkpoint, not the trigger.

- **2026-06-10 — Auto-continue phases (Jason).** After a phase closes cleanly (self-audit PASS, no fatal/major blocker requiring Jason), automatically proceed to the next phase. Non-major decisions: the agent decides per its own recommendation and LOGS the decision; only genuinely major decisions (story-changing, scope-changing, spend-changing) wait for Jason.

- **2026-06-10 — Page budgets are FILL targets (Jason; refined 2026-06-10).** A stated content-page limit x is a RANGE target: x ≤ content ≤ x+0.5 pages (e.g., INFOCOM 9pp content + 1pp references: content between 9 and 9.5). Never under x; up to half a page spilling into the reference page is fine. For a 10-page-total paper, references must not exceed 30 entries.
- **2026-06-10 — Post-revision full-document logic pass (Jason).** After every major revision cycle, perform a front-to-back logical-consistency read of the whole paper from a fresh reader's perspective (clean-context reviewer): logic holes, forward references, claims updated in one place but not another, narrative discontinuities.

- **2026-06-11 — Iterate to the acceptance bar; deliver only polished, complete results (Jason).** Keep optimizing an experiment — method, implementation, hyperparameters of OUR method, experiment design — until it reaches the pre-registered acceptance bar; half-working experiment runs are not paper material and are not delivered. Experiments that have passed validation are FROZEN (no churn on their recorded results). Hand off for Jason's acceptance only when the full result set is complete and has survived the self-review pass below: what reaches Jason must be finished work. Operational boundary (per skills 03/06 and the EAGER D35→D38 precedent, which Jason ratified): "keep trying" means fixing bugs, strengthening our own method, and improving experiment design — never weakening baselines, shopping seeds, silently dropping unfavorable instances, or moving an acceptance bar without a logged, owner-ratified decision entry. When reality contradicts the expectation, classify bug-vs-finding with evidence: bugs get fixed and rerun; genuine findings get characterized (regime map, mechanism, boundary) and escalated for a framing ruling — a characterized finding presented on our terms IS a polished result, not a failure.
- **2026-06-11 — Post-result self-review (Jason).** After every result lands, run a detailed self-review before handoff: anything imperfect? anything improvable (coverage, statistical strength, presentation, runtime)? If yes, continue refining the experiment and re-run the review. Delivered results must have survived this pass; the review's checklist and outcomes are recorded with the phase evidence.
